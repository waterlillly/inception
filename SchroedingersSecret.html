The data may or may not be private — until observed.

Welcome to the experimental playground where containers, code, and curiosity collide. Here, we collect "personal data" (mostly for fun and learning), but like Schrödinger's cat, you can never be 100% sure what's happening until you peek. Comments, cookies, and mysterious log files may exist in a state of superposition — sometimes recorded, sometimes not. Honestly, we're just as clueless as you are. Think of this as the quantum version of a privacy policy: partly real, partly theoretical, entirely fun. Dive in at your own risk, and remember — your secrets might be both safe and exposed… until observed.

 

Who We Are

My website address is: https://lbaumeis.42.fr. I am a single project with multiple personalities on too much caffeine who occasionally remember to sleep.

What Personal Data Is Collected and Why

I don't really want your data — I barely know what to do with my own. But the internet insists, so:

Comments: If you comment, we will store your words forever (or until the heat death of the universe).

Cookies: I like to bake cookies, and your browser does too. They may contain traces of login sessions, chocolate chips, and questionable PHP.

Analytics: Sometimes we stare at graphs that tell us people visit the site. We're not really sure why.

Comments

When you leave a comment, we capture your IP address, browser agent, and (maybe soon — still in development) your mood. If you're using Gravatar, your mysterious internet face will also appear.

Media

If you upload images, please don't leave your vacation GPS tags in there — unless you're inviting all of us to come visit. In which case… thanks (but no, thanks).

Contact Forms

If you send a message, it will probably sit in my inbox until one of my personalities either remembers the password or gets a random burst of motivation. It won't be used for marketing, just for awkward replies at 3 AM.

Cookies

Login cookies: last two days.

Screen preference cookies: last a year.

Chocolate chip cookies: last about 30 seconds in the Basecamp at 42.

Embedded Content from Other Websites

If I embed YouTube videos or other stuff, be prepared and expect the unexpected. Other sites may track you, but hey — no risk, no fun.

Who We Share Your Data With

Nobody.

We don't go outside — we're not social — and basically we don't even know anyone we could (or would like to) share your data with. You're safe!

How Long Your Data Is Retained

Comments: Indefinitely (a- because nostalgia is a thing, b- because we'll probably just forget about it).

User accounts: Until you rage-quit coding.

Error logs: Probably forever, because debugging is eternal for at least one of my personalities.

What Rights You Have Over Your Data

You can request an export of your data, or tell me to delete it. Some of us might cry a little, but it'll be done eventually (unless we forget — in which case, sorry, please try again later)

Where Your Data Is Sent

Visitor comments may be checked through a spam-detection service that's smarter than most individuals I've come across so far.
